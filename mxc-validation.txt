=== Enclawed under MXC Bubblewrap, firewall enforcement (sudo) ===

Same shape as above but with the bundled network policy applied
(defaultPolicy=block, enforcementMode=firewall, allowedHosts=
[api.anthropic.com, 127.0.0.1]). Ran as `sudo node ...`.

  exit=255
  stderr: Bubblewrap: network policy error: iptables -A MXC-enclawed-
          bubblewrap- -d 2607:6bc0::10 -j ACCEPT failed: iptables
          v1.8.10 (nf_tables): host/network '2607:6bc0::10' not found

  → MXC bug, not config-shape. `iptables` (IPv4 only) cannot accept
    the IPv6 address `2607:6bc0::10` that api.anthropic.com resolves
    to via its AAAA records. Filed upstream as
    microsoft/mxc#479 (Bubblewrap network firewall mode rejects
    IPv6-resolved hosts). Workarounds documented in the README:
    enforcementMode=proxy, or rely on enclawed's own egress
    allowlist with defaultPolicy=allow.
